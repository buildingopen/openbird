# Template: Escalation Notice to the Airline

Use this letter when:
- The airline has rejected your claim with an incorrect legal argument, OR
- The airline has not responded within 14–30 days of your initial claim

This letter gives the airline one final opportunity to resolve the matter before you file with enforcement authorities. It is short and firm — its purpose is to signal that you know your rights and are prepared to use them.
